SaraCell Platform Information

About SaraCell:
SaraCell is a college student re-commerce platform.
It allows verified college students to buy and sell used items within their campus.

Who Can Use:
Only registered college students using official college email IDs.

How to Buy:
1. Login using college email.
2. Search for a product.
3. View details and contact seller.
4. Meet safely within campus.
5. Complete transaction.

How to Sell:
1. Login to SaraCell.
2. Click "Sell Item".
3. Upload product image.
4. Enter price and description.
5. Post listing.
6. Wait for buyer response.

Safety Rules:
- No illegal items.
- No fake listings.
- Meet in public campus locations.
- Report suspicious users.

Policies:
- SaraCell does not process payments.
- It only connects buyers and sellers.
- Violating rules may result in account suspension.
